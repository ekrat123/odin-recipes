Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich fillings such as meat (often ground beef or sausage), cheese (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), and tomato or b√©chamel sauce. It's baked until golden and bubbly, creating a hearty, flavorful casserole. Lasagna is popular for its comforting taste and satisfying layers of flavors and textures.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef (or Italian sausage)
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes)
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Dried basil and oregano

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce
  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Let it rest
